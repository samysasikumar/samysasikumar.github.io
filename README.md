### 📊 US Domestic Travel Analysis — WA & HI
A data science project analyzing US domestic travel patterns across Washington and Hawaii from 2019–2025 — capturing pre-COVID baseline, pandemic collapse, and full recovery. Applied linear regression in Excel to project future travel demand. Submitted as a TSA (Technology Student Association) school competition entry.
- **Tech:** Excel, Linear Regression, Public Dataset
- **Key Finding:** Both states showed full recovery post-COVID with projected continued growth, with Hawaii and Washington recovering at notably different rates due to their distinct travel profiles.
