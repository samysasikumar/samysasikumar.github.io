About

The Story So Far

I'm a high school student in Redmond, WA with a clear goal: study Computer Science in college and build things that matter.

I'm a member of my school's Computer Science Club and Girls Who Code. Through a collaboration between Girls Who Code and Coding for Medicine, I'm helping build a real app for our school's health office — experiencing the full software development cycle the way it works in professional environments.

Through TSA (Technology Student Association), I competed in the Data Science project event — analyzing six years of US domestic travel data across Washington and Hawaii, capturing the full COVID impact and recovery, and applying linear regression to project future trends.

I also launched a real business (Samy's Crochet 🧶), built a live website for it, and designed a text-based adventure game in Python.

My plan includes AP CSA, AP Calculus, AP Statistics, and a summer internship before senior year.
